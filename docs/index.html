Wikidata Top Card Game


Gameplay:
Click a property on the card. Compare with the computer's card that appears. If you guessed correctly, you win!

To win you should correctly guess whether the property you selected will have a higher or lower value than the same property on the computer's card. Click on an existing card again to get the next card.
